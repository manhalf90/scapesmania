Project Description & Features

ScapesMania is a gaming ecosystem for players that enjoy a captivating story, engaging mini-games and customization of their own house and garden, built in the Casual Art's setting.

Throughout the gameplay players will learn more about the town and its folks, will decorate their own house and land and participate in the local contest to become the best creator.

Reputation
By completing personal errands for the townsfolk players will improve their relationship with them to uncover new story bits or additional gameplay features.
Customize Your Land
Players will have various tools to improve and decorate their homes and land.
Mini-Games
To earn resources for new decorations or story bits players will play engaging mini-games such as match-3.
Story
The town and its residents all have their unique stories and secrets to uncover.
